"FOODS OF LPU"

There are both Veg & Non-Veg mess facility available in Lovely Professional University. Multiple cuisines made available for the students of LPU as there are huge diversity of students available in LPU campus. For your information International cuisines are also available keeping in view that there are huge number of International students are available inside campus. Domestic cuisines are like North Indian / South Indian / Rajasthani / Haryanvi / Gujrati / Eastern / North-Eastern. There are following options available in LPU Mess : 1) Standard Mess - with four meals 2) Standard Mess - with three meals (i.e. without lunch) 3) Basic Mess - with three meals (i.e. without evening snacks) 4) Basic Mess - with two meals (i.e. without lunch and evening Snacks) 5) Ala Carte - Choice of Dishes 6) Ala Carte - Choice of Cuisines & Dishes Prices are varying according to your choices. Not only the Mess food, there is multiple other food options available inside LPU campus like around 400 Food Kiosk, branded Food Giants like Cafe Coffee Day, Dominos etc. I hope your query is resolved.
